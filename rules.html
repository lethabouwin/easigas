Rules

To qualify:

  • You must be a South African resident over 18 years old
  • Provide proof of purchase to EasiGas for validation
  • Once approved, you'll receive login details for claiming your voucher

How To Register:

Registration is free and voluntary. EasiGas rewards your participation.

How to claim your voucher:

  • Log in to your EasiGas account to view and claim vouchers.
  • Terms include points' expiration after a year and their non-convertibility to cash. Redeem points for various digital vouchers through the platform.
  • Order rewards by logging into the incentive website, where your wallet will accumulate winnings. Choose and claim vouchers from a catalogue, delivered within 7 working days.
  • You'll be rewarded with points three days after redeeming them for a digital voucher. Communication will be via email regarding your rewards and voucher codes.

How do I earn points?

  • For buying a 9kg, 14kg and 19kg EasiGas Cylinder you win a R50 voucher
  • For buying a 48kg EasiGas Cylinder. you win a R100 voucher

How do I get my voucher?

  • Your Voucher will be sent to you via email or sms.
  • Remember to check your Spam/Junk folder for emails. Spend points within a year to avoid expiration.

How do I redeem my voucher?

  • You will need to take your voucher code to the store and redeem your voucher.

For queries, refer to this information or contact the UwinIwin Helpdesk on 011 557 5700. Responses may take up to 24 hours.
